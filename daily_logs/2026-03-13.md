## 🧠 What I Learned / Key Takeaways
- **Decoupling architectures** improves resilience and scalability in distributed systems
  - Services like **Amazon SQS, SNS, and EventBridge** enable loosely coupled communication between application components
- Messaging services act as **buffers or event routers**, preventing cascading failures between services
- **Auto Scaling + Load Balancing** work together to maintain availability and distribute workload across instances
- AWS compute services exist on a **spectrum of abstraction**:
  - EC2 → Containers → Serverless (Lambda)
- Containers provide lightweight, portable environments compared to traditional virtual machines
