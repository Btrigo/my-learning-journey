## 🧠 What I Learned / Key Takeaways
- CAF People vs. Business trap: "company-wide change management" = People perspective, NOT Business — answered this wrong in the assessment and locked it in
- 7 Rs distinction: Rehost moves anything (physical, virtual, cloud) as-is; Relocate is platform-specific (e.g., VMware SDDC → VMware Cloud on AWS)
- DMS vs. SCT: DMS moves the data, SCT converts the schema — heterogeneous migrations need both (SCT runs first)
- DataSync = automation/scheduling/acceleration; Transfer Family = SFTP/FTP/FTPS protocol support — these are not interchangeable
- Migration Hub is free and spans the full migration lifecycle from discovery through execution
- WAF pillar mapping reinforced: multi-AZ = Reliability, rightsizing = Cost Optimization, serverless architecture reduces Operational Excellence overhead
- AWS X-Ray is the tracing/debugging tool for serverless architectures
- AWS Amplify = rapid full-stack app development (auth, API, storage, hosting, CI/CD)
