## Key Takeaways
- IaC = Operational Excellence ("perform operations as code") — not Performance Efficiency
- CAF Governance is broader than just compliance — it covers orchestrating cloud initiatives, managing risk, maximizing business value, and aligning IT strategy to business outcomes
- CloudTrail and AWS Config are different tools: CloudTrail tracks API calls/events; Config tracks resource configuration state over time — use Config to prove configuration history
- Managed databases (RDS, DynamoDB) = AWS handles patching, backups, HA; Unmanaged (EC2 + DB) = you handle everything above the hardware layer
- CAF transformation phases in order: Envision → Align → Launch → Scale
- Sustainability pillar uses Spot Instances to consume spare capacity and reduce energy waste — not Performance Efficiency
- On multi-pillar WAF questions, read why a service is being used, not just what service it is
