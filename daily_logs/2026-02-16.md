## üß† What I Learned / Key Takeaways
- The AWS Well-Architected Framework provides structured guidance across 6 pillars: Operational Excellence, Security, Reliability, Performance Efficiency, Cost Optimization, and Sustainability.
- Each pillar contains Design Principles, Definitions, Best Practices, and supporting Resources.
- Operational Excellence emphasizes treating infrastructure as code and making small, reversible changes.
- Security design principles revolve around least privilege, traceability, defense in depth, and protecting data in transit and at rest.
- Reliability focuses heavily on automation, horizontal scaling, recovery testing, and eliminating single points of failure.
- Performance Efficiency encourages serverless architectures, experimentation, and global deployments.
- Cost Optimization centers on consumption-based models, financial visibility, and avoiding undifferentiated heavy lifting.
- The AWS Well-Architected Tool is an auditing tool that evaluates workloads against the framework and generates improvement reports.
- CloudWatch is primarily used for monitoring metrics and alarms, while CloudTrail is focused on auditing API activity and changes.
